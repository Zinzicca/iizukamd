Adult Patients

If you are an adult patient and I have seen, you recently or awaiting surgery (after June 2023) you may book your follow up at TESS or Oakville (via phone) by calling my secretary and leaving me a message. They will not immediately book you a follow up on the phone until your request can be processed as they will not have a copy of your chart. This process may take up to two weeks. Patients awaiting surgery may contact Dr Iizuka via OTEC with my surgery counsellor

Kids

For kids, parents can request to be seen at either St Joseph’s Hospital or TESS. You will not be able to book you an appointment on the phone immediately until your request can be processed. The appointment may not be given to you for up to two weeks. If I have not seen you or your child in the last two years please request another consult request via their family doctors or optometrist to TESS, St Joseph's eye clinic or OTEC (adults only).

Requesting Medical Records

If you would like a copy of your chart from the Bloor office, then you must mail me a cheque for $40 to the attention of Dr Iizuka at 30 the Queensway (include address of St Joseph’s hospital). The cheque should be made out to Iizuka Medicine Professional Corporation Please indicate an email address where your chart can be sent electronically OR include a self addressed stamped envelope with the appropriate postage. Please be aware that electronic communication may not be secure if you send me a non confidential email address.
